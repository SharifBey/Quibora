Who We Are ? We are students from Central Asia who came to Taiwan in age of 18 seeking high quality education. After living here (in Taiwan) for several years, we have experienced a lot of different things, such as meeting friends from different cultures. It is a unique experience where you can learn a lot. By sharing your own experience in English you can improve your language profieciency and by listening you can learn about different cultures and how they live.

What We Want ? Our mission is to create a safe tunnel and guide students from Central Asia to countries that can provide quality education and opportunity. We consider ourselves to be one of the luckiest people who got an opportunity to study in one of the most developed country in the world. We feel that we owe a lot to the luck, we would like to pay back this debt by providing such an opportunity to youth of Central Asia who deserves the best quality education.
